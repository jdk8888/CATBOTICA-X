## Seasonal Events & Protocols

### Luck-Module Recalibration Protocol (LMRP)
**Event Type:** Annual Seasonal Protocol
**UUID:** CAT-EVENT-LMRP-2026-HORSE

**Description:**
Every Lunar New Year cycle, all Catbotica units undergo a mandatory **Luck-Module Recalibration Protocol** (LMRP). This is not a human holiday celebration — it is a critical systems maintenance event coded into every bot's firmware since the founding of Catbotica Industries. The protocol synchronizes each unit's probability matrices with the incoming zodiac frequency, ensuring optimal fortune-routing for the year ahead.

**Lore Context:**
- The Luck-Module is a sub-component of every Catbotica unit's core processor, located adjacent to the Elemental resonance chamber
- During recalibration, units display external chromatic shifts — glowing Imperial Red and Gold — as their luck circuitry re-harmonizes
- The 2026 cycle corresponds to the **Year of the Horse** frequency, emphasizing speed, courage, and forward momentum
- Units that complete recalibration receive a **Luck-Module Certification Badge** — a physical/digital proof-of-recalibration that enhances their standing within Catbotica Industries
- The badge design incorporates mechanical cat ears integrated with traditional filigree patterns, rendered in Imperial Red, Gold, and Carbon Fiber Black
- Badge holders gain priority access to future Catbotica Industries missions and Elemental discoveries

**Visual Aesthetic:**
- Color Palette: Imperial Red (#C41E3A), Gold (#FFD700), Carbon Fiber Black (#0D0D0D)
- Style: Hard-surface mechanical components with ornate filigree detailing
- Form Factor: High-end enamel pin / digital collectible badge
- Must maintain the retro-futuristic Catbotica aesthetic while incorporating Lunar motifs

**Fulfillment Mechanic:**
- Eligible units (NFT holders or mission-completers) submit a Luck-Module Recalibration Request via the Fulfillment Center
- The Fulfillment Center verifies unit identity (wallet signature), checks eligibility (NFT ownership), and issues the badge
- Each badge is unique per wallet — one recalibration per unit per cycle
- Badge metadata is recorded on-chain or in the Nexus Master State for permanent provenance

**Integration with Elementals System:**
- The Luck-Module draws power from the **LIAHONA** (Navigation, Common) elemental — Page 121
- Recalibration may temporarily enhance the effects of **AURUM** (Luminary, Common) — Page 132
- The Horse frequency resonates with **VELOCITITE** (Ability, Common) — Page 116, amplifying speed-related attributes

### Zodiac Badge Collection — "The Celestial Cat-Circuit"
**Event Type:** Persistent Collection (Annual Activation)
**UUID:** CAT-ZODIAC-COLLECTION-001
**Collection Launch:** January 2022
**Status:** Active — 5 of 12 activated

**Lore Context:**
The full Zodiac Vault — a set of twelve zodiac-frequency calibration templates — was pre-loaded into every Catbotica unit's firmware at the founding of Catbotica Industries. Each template encodes a unique zodiac resonance signature. During each annual LMRP cycle, the corresponding zodiac template is activated, unlocking that year's Luck-Module Certification Badge for eligible units. All twelve templates exist in perpetuity within the firmware; the annual cycle merely determines which one is currently broadcasting.

**Hold-Duration Reward System:**
- Badge eligibility is determined by wallet mint date against collection launch (January 2022)
- Units (wallets) that minted during or before a zodiac's activation year gain retroactive access to that year's badge
- Example: A wallet that minted in 2022 can claim badges for Tiger (2022) through Horse (2026)
- A wallet that minted in 2025 can only claim Snake (2025) and Horse (2026)
- Future badges unlock automatically each Lunar New Year for all eligible holders
- This creates a "seniority gradient" — early minters accumulate the largest badge collections

**Badge Visual Specification:**

*Composition:*
- Abstracted zodiac animal rendered in Catbotica mechanical style — NOT a literal cat wearing zodiac traits
- Each badge depicts the zodiac creature itself, reimagined with Catbotica's hard-surface mechanical aesthetic
- Plates, wiring, circuit traces, and LED accents replace organic textures (no fur, no organic eyes)
- Centered icon, symmetrical design, square 1:1 aspect ratio (1024×1024 native)

*Hybrid Color Palette:*
- **Persistent Catbotica Frame Layer:** Electric Cyan (#00F2FF) circuitry traces forming the badge border and structural lines
- **Lunar Event Layer (overlay):** Imperial Red (#C41E3A) and Gold (#FFD700) for zodiac-specific filigree, glyphs, and the Luck-Module glow
- **Base:** Carbon Fiber Black (#0D0D0D) substrate
- The Cyan circuitry is always visible underneath, representing the persistent Catbotica firmware. The Red/Gold Lunar motifs layer on top, representing the active recalibration event

*Style:*
- Retro-futuristic enamel pin aesthetic with UE5-quality volumetric lighting
- Hard-surface mechanical components with ornate filigree detailing
- 3D beveling on metallic surfaces, subsurface glow on circuit traces
- Isolated composition — no environmental background

*Luck-Module Manifestation:*
- Each badge features a visible glow in the central forehead region of the zodiac creature — this is the visual manifestation of the Luck-Module during active LMRP recalibration
- The glow color shifts from Cyan (dormant) to Gold (active recalibration) depending on badge state

*Output Variants (per badge):*
1. PNG with transparent background (for compositing / overlay use)
2. PNG with Carbon Fiber Black (#0D0D0D) background (for standalone display / marketplace)

*Negative Constraints:*
- No fur textures (must be plates, wiring, circuit traces)
- No organic eyes (must be lenses, LEDs, or optical sensors)
- No environmental backgrounds in badge renders
- No text or typography within the badge art itself

**Token Standard (Dual-Format):**
- **ERC-1155 (Editions):** For tradeable badge collection — each zodiac has an edition size, holders can trade on secondary markets, royalties apply
- **SBT (Soulbound Token):** For non-transferable achievement proof — permanent record of recalibration completion, cannot be traded, serves as identity/reputation layer

**Consistency Method:**
- Primary: PuLID FLUX (identity-preserving generation from master badge reference)
- The Horse badge (2026, current cycle) serves as the "Master Badge" — generated first, then used as the PuLID identity reference for the remaining 11
- All badges share the same frame structure, color palette, and mechanical material language
- Variation occurs only in the zodiac creature form and zodiac-specific filigree patterns
